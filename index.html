Front Tooth Bonding Before After

12 year old patient with old failing bonding and caries on front tooth, creating an esthetic issue. Old bonding and caries was removed and a new bonding was placed, matching the color of the tooth and blending the line so that it is not visible.

Full Mouth Reconstruction Before After

Patient with numerous decayed and fractured teeth, as well as a clenching/grinding habit that contributed to further destruction and loss of vertical dimension. The teeth were restored with crowns and the bite was balanced.
